About

Senior PHP & WordPress developer in Nepal — and a shipping partner you can trust.

I'm Umesh Ghimire. For more than a decade I've split my time between writing production PHP and JavaScript code and running the engineering teams that ship it. Based in Maharajganj, Kathmandu — working with clients worldwide.

My story

Twelve-plus years of shipping software in Nepal.

I started my career in 2012 building CodeIgniter and OpenCart e-commerce sites at E-prabidhi. From there, full-stack work at Sofos and telecom-scale PHP at Unifun shaped how I think about reliability — when your code runs on a carrier billing platform, "works on my machine" stops being good enough.

In 2017 I joined ThemeGrill, then helped build MantraBrain into a respected WordPress product company, where I led the engineering teams behind flagship plugins like Yatra, Sikshya LMS and the User Registration Plugin. Returning to ThemeGrill as Project Manager from 2023–2025, I owned sprint planning, hiring and product strategy for a portfolio used by hundreds of thousands of sites.

Today I take on senior freelance and retainer engagements: WordPress plugin development, Laravel SaaS, code rescue work, and fractional-PM roles for teams that need a senior hand to keep delivery predictable.
