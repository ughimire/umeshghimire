## Laravel vs WordPress — when to pick Laravel

Both are PHP. The real question is shape: WordPress is a content tool you extend; Laravel is a framework you assemble into an application. The further your project is from "documents and pages" and the closer to "users doing work in a custom UI", the more Laravel earns its keep.

Pick Laravel when you have a bespoke data model with non-trivial business rules, a real REST or GraphQL API as a first-class deliverable, multi-tenant or workspace logic, billing and subscriptions, or a team that will grow and benefit from explicit conventions. See the in-depth [WordPress vs Laravel guide](/blog/wordpress-vs-laravel/).
