Why hire a WooCommerce specialist over a generic web developer?

WooCommerce is deceptive. A demo store goes up in an afternoon. A production store with real customers, real payments and real edge cases is an entirely different animal — and that's where generic web developers get into trouble. Tax rules, refund flows, partial fulfilment, subscription renewals, gateway timeouts, multi-currency rounding, EU VAT, GST, and a dozen jurisdictional quirks each have a "correct" WooCommerce way and a wrong way. Picking wrong creates silent bugs that surface at quarter-end audit time.

A specialist has seen these traps before and routes around them. That's what you're paying for.

Nepal payment gateways I integrate

If you're selling to Nepali customers, your store needs Nepali rails. The gateways I integrate as first-class WooCommerce payment methods include:

  • eSewa — Nepal's largest digital wallet. Standard checkout, refunds, transaction reconciliation.
  • Khalti — popular wallet with mobile-first checkout. Web checkout and mobile SDK integration.
  • Fonepay — bank-direct payment rail covering most Nepali banks. QR and account-debit flows.
  • ConnectIPS — direct bank-to-bank account debit, useful for higher-value transactions.
  • PayPal & Stripe — for international customers, configured alongside Nepal gateways with smart routing.
