## Why hire a WooCommerce specialist over a generic web developer?

WooCommerce is deceptive. A demo store goes up in an afternoon. A production store with real customers, real payments and real edge cases is an entirely different animal. Tax rules, refund flows, partial fulfilment, subscription renewals, gateway timeouts, multi-currency rounding, EU VAT, GST, and a dozen jurisdictional quirks each have a "correct" WooCommerce way and a wrong way. A specialist has seen these traps before and routes around them.

## WooCommerce services I offer
