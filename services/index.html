FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to hire a WordPress developer in Nepal?

Most of my engagements run between USD 2,000 and USD 40,000 depending on scope. Small fixes and audits start lower; full plugin builds and Laravel SaaS projects sit in the middle to upper range. I'll share a fixed quote after a short scoping call.

Do you work with clients outside Nepal?

Yes — most of my clients are based outside Nepal. I work async-friendly and have overlap with US, UK, EU, India and Australian time zones.

Can you take over a legacy WordPress or Laravel project?

Absolutely. Code rescue and audit work is a sizeable part of what I do. I'll deliver a written report covering security, performance, architecture and a prioritized fix plan.

What's your typical engagement model?

Three options: fixed-scope projects, monthly retainers with reserved capacity, or one-off audits and consulting. We'll pick whichever fits your timeline and budget best.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. I happily sign mutual NDAs before any sensitive details are shared, and I'm comfortable working under existing client contracts and IP-assignment agreements.

What's your time zone availability?

I'm based in Kathmandu (UTC+5:45). Typical working hours overlap comfortably with India, Middle East, EU, UK and Australia. For US clients I take 1–2 calls per week outside normal hours.

Ready to scope your project?

Tell me what you're building. You'll get an honest reply with scope, timeline and budget within one business day.
